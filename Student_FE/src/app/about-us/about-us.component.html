Our History

Mireille, a driven student at the University of Pretoria, embarked on her academic journey with high hopes, only to encounter the challenge of outdated textbooks during her first year. Frustrated by the lack of a reliable platform for students to buy and sell their books, she envisioned a solution where the exchange of knowledge could be seamless. Fuelled by her determination, Mireille started connecting fellow students by facilitating textbook sales through bulletin boards and online groups. Recognizing her knack for this, she transformed her efforts into a thriving venture called The Book Market.


Our Progression

What began as a small initiative blossomed into a comprehensive hub, catering not only to the academic needs of students but also offering essential lab equipment and stationery. The Book Market became a go-to destination, ensuring juniors had access to the right materials while providing seniors with an effortless way to sell their textbooks. As the business grew, Mireille expanded her offerings, introducing personalized lab coats adorned with students' embroidered names and the school emblem. This thoughtful touch not only elevated the student experience but also reflected Mireille's commitment to enhancing education at the university.


The Book Market's journey, born out of a student's passion and perseverance, stands as a testament to the power of bridging gaps within the academic community.

Our Future Hopes

In the future, The Book Market envisions a mobile application designed to enhance user experience. The app will offer a straightforward interface, allowing students to easily browse textbooks, lab equipment, and stationery. With a few taps, users can find the editions they need and receive personalized recommendations based on their courses and preferences. Alongside this, a streamlined delivery system is in development, ensuring prompt delivery of orders to students' doorsteps. The Book Market also plans to introduce a tracking feature, enabling real-time monitoring of orders from packing to delivery. These developments aim to simplify the process of accessing academic materials, making it more convenient for students. Stay tuned for updates on these enhancements to your educational journey.
